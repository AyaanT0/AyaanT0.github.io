CitySearch

CitySearch is a concept app promoting inclusivity and accessibility for disabled people. It features locations, such as restaurants, that have made accomodations for disabled people (such as ramps at the entrance). It also features a user review system, where users can rate the accessibility and accomodations of the location. We have also devised an algorithm for the rating system.
